About Us

Our mission is to make high-fidelity marine carbon dioxide removal (mCDR) forecasting accessible, fast, and scientifically robust. We are building an AI-enabled forecasting stack that couples GPU-accelerated ocean and biogeochemistry models with kilometer-scale atmospheric predictions, enabling users to forecast CO2 uptake and durability decades into the future. By unifying advances in physics-based modeling and machine learning, the CDR Forecasting Stack brings next-generation carbon forecasting within reach of a broad community of researchers, practitioners, and decision-makers.
